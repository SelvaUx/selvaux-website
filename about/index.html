Who I Am

The Builder

I'm Selva Pandi — Selva.Ux online. An ECE student who decided that waiting to be qualified was a worse plan than just building the thing. So I built an AI assistant. Then another version. Then seven. Then I built a custom OS for it. Then a fiber fault detection engine. Then a voice file manager. The list doesn't stop because the curiosity doesn't stop.

My engineering philosophy is simple: offline first, privacy always, real hardware required. I don't trust solutions that work only in ideal conditions. I build for the messy, low-bandwidth, real world — where people need technology that actually functions.

Beyond code and circuits, I find the same obsessive attention to detail in everything — anime arcs that land perfectly, physics thought experiments that keep me up at night, and the quiet fire of building your own Iron Man world before getting a driver's license.
